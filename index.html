Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer for new small businesses?

We offer a full suite of services for new businesses, including business entity selection advice, bookkeeping setup, payroll services, and strategic tax planning to ensure you start on a solid financial footing.

What do I need to bring for my individual tax appointment?

We recommend bringing all relevant tax documents such as W-2s, 1099s, records of any other income, information on deductions and credits (e.g., mortgage interest, student loan interest, charitable donations), and a copy of last year's tax return.

How are your fees structured?

Our fees are based on the complexity of the work involved. We believe in transparency and will provide you with a clear price quote after an initial consultation to understand your specific needs. We pride ourselves on offering exceptional value without hidden costs.
